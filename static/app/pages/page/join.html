Join Us

Learn, Collaborate and Create Research Talks on Influential Papers in CS

We're a thousand strong community who have been collaboratively creating research talks on multiple topics in computer science for almost an year. Our 150+ videos in more than 10 global languages have attracted about 70,000 views and 1,000 subscribers to date.

There are thousands of important research results that deserve more exposure. However, they’re in hard to understand format and many times behind the paywall. Our goal is to make the world's research more accessible, by collaboratively creating 5-minute short talks on influential papers. While creating these talks, not only you’ll learn about cutting-edge research, collaborate and discuss, but your work will be viewed by people around the world.

Come join Stanford Scholar Initiative and help create talks in any language or contribute in any capacity. Anyone can join in, from high school to PhD students, in any area of computer science or technology.

If you have any questions, do not hesitate to email Stanford Scholar Team. And please spread the word. Let's make it big!
