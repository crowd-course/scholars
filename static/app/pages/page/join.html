Learn, Collaborate and Create Research Talks on Influential Papers in CS

We're a 500 people strong community, who have been working hard to collaboratively create research talks in multiple topics, in multiple languages. Our 150+ videos have so far attracted more than 15,000 views. In the spirit of making research and knowledge more accessible, we're starting this new effort!

There are thousands and thousands of research papers that go unread, some are behind paywall, some are hard for non-researchers to read and understand - besides writing papers, researchers are unable to spend a lot of time making that valuable knowledge accessible. But we can help!

Our goal is to make world's research more accessible, by collaboratively creating short 5 mins talks on influential papers, starting with CS. Not only you'll learn a lot, but your work will be viewed by people around the world.

By signing up below, you're gonna be part of the STANFORD SCHOLAR platform, where you can collaborate and help science. This is the beginning of a high impact effort, and hope you'll join us!
